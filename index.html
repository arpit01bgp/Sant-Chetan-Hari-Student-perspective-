Welcome to Sant Chetan Hari Sarswati Vidya Mandir


Introduction

Sant Chetan Hari Sarswati Vidya Mandir is a beacon of education located in the heart of Satghara, Habibpur, Bhagalpur. Established in 2008, School has been dedicated to providing quality education to the students of school's community for over a decade.

Overview

  • Classes: About 15(Arun to X)

  • Vandana Hall: A Spacious Hall for Various Events and Gatherings.

  • Student Population: Over 380 Bright Minds and Counting.

  • Dedicated Faculty: A Team of About 20 Highly Qualified Teachers.

  • Boys Toilets: 4 Well-Maintained Toilets for male Students.

  • Classrooms: Well-Conditioned Spaces for Optimal Learning.

  • Location: Situated in the Serene Ambiance of Satghara, Habibpur, Bhagalpur.

Facilities

  • Water Supply: With About 15 Functional Taps, School Ensure Access to Clean Water at All Times.

  • Hygiene: Our Toilets and Facilities are Kept in Impeccable Condition.

  • Playground: A Safe and Vibrant Space for Students to Engage in Physical Activities.

  • Computer Lab: Equipped with 5 Computers Running on Windows 7.

  • Specialized Subjects: School Excel in Providing Quality Education in Science (Biology, Chemistry, Physics), Mathematics, Social Sciences, and More.

  • Library: A Treasure Trove of Knowledge for Inquisitive Minds.

  • Low Fee Structure: Ensuring Education is Accessible to All.
